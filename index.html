25+ Years of Service
Welcome to Our Center

A Place of Peace and Spiritual Enrichment

Established in 2001, Masjid Al-Noor has been serving as the focal point for Muslims in our local community. We strive to provide a welcoming atmosphere for all who wish to learn about Islam, perform daily prayers, and participate in community building.

Our core mission is to foster spiritual development, maintain Islamic values, and build positive relationships with other faith groups to promote understanding, peace, and harmony.

Community & Unity

What We Offer

Mosque Services

Offering religious, educational, and social services to support the spiritual and daily needs of our congregation.

Quran Academy

Daily classes for children and adults to learn Tajweed, memorization (Hifz), and the understanding of the Holy Quran.
